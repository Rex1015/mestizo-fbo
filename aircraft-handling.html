Handling Services in Detail

Secure, supervised ramp parking for short and long-term stays. Priority positioning available for VIP and time-sensitive operations at Sangley Point.

  • Priority positioning

  • Enclosed protection
  • Maintenance access

  • Security monitored

GPU & Towing

  • GPU connection

  • All aircraft types

HOW IT WORKS

Our Handling Process

Seamless coordination from arrival to departure.

01

Pre-Arrival Notice

Submit your ETA, aircraft type, and parking requirements. We prepare your designated spot in advance.

02

Arrival & Marshalling

Our certified marshallers guide your aircraft to the designated parking position safely and efficiently.

03

Ground Services

GPU connection, chocking, and any additional ground services performed immediately upon parking.

04

Departure Ready

Aircraft repositioned, pre-departure checks coordinated, and full handling report provided on request.
